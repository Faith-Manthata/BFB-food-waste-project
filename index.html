Database Design

Our system uses a well-structured SQLite database with proper normalization to efficiently manage food waste data across the supply chain.

Database Schema

The database includes at least 3 tables with properly defined primary keys, foreign keys, and relationships:

Users Table

  • user_id (PK)
  • username
  • password_hash
  • email
  • role
  • created_at

Inventory Table

  • item_id (PK)
  • item_name
  • category
  • quantity
  • expiry_date
  • supplier_id (FK)
  • added_date

Waste Records Table

  • waste_id (PK)
  • item_id (FK)
  • quantity_wasted
  • reason
  • date_recorded
  • recorded_by (FK)

About Our Mission

Food waste is a critical global issue, with approximately one-third of all food produced for human consumption lost or wasted. Our platform addresses this problem directly within the supply chain.

About BFB Food Waste Project

Our Vision

We envision a world where food waste is minimized throughout the supply chain, from farm to fork. Our platform empowers businesses with the data and tools needed to make informed decisions that reduce waste and improve sustainability.

How We Address Food Waste

Food waste occurs at multiple points in the supply chain:

  • Production: Crops not meeting aesthetic standards
  • Processing: Inefficient processing methods
  • Distribution: Spoilage during transport and storage
  • Retail: Overstocking and expiration
  • Consumption: Household and restaurant waste

Our Technology

Using SQLite database technology, we've created a robust system that:

  • Tracks inventory in real-time
  • Monitors expiration dates
  • Predicts waste patterns using historical data
  • Generates actionable insights for waste reduction

Database Architecture

Our SQLite database follows proper normalization principles with:

  • 3+ Normal Form: Eliminates data redundancy
  • Primary Keys: Unique identifiers for each record
  • Foreign Keys: Maintains referential integrity
  • Relationships: Clear 1-to-many relationships between entities

Our Approach

Data-Driven

Using analytics and machine learning to identify waste patterns and predict future waste.

User-Centric

Intuitive interfaces designed for both managers and employees in the food supply chain.

Sustainable

Focused on creating long-term solutions that benefit both businesses and the environment.
